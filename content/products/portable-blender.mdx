## Why Portable Blenders Are Blowing Up

TikTok has been obsessed with portable blenders lately, and after testing this one for two weeks, I get it. The convenience factor is real.

## Performance

The 200W motor surprised me. It actually crushes ice — not instantly, but definitely gets there. Frozen fruit? No problem. The key is cutting ingredients into smaller chunks first.

Battery life is the standout feature. 15 full blends on a single charge means you can take it on a weekend trip without bringing the USB-C cable.

## Daily Use
I've been making morning smoothies at my desk instead of in the kitchen. Blend → drink from the same cup → rinse the blades → done. It genuinely saves 5 minutes of cleanup vs a regular blender.

## The Tradeoffs
It takes 3-4 hours to charge fully, so keep it plugged in overnight. And with hard ingredients like frozen strawberries, it gets loud — not apartment-friendly at 6 AM.

## Bottom Line
For $29.99, this is worth it if you make smoothies regularly and value convenience over raw power. Not replacing your Vitamix, but definitely replacing your "I'll make a smoothie when I get home" excuses.
