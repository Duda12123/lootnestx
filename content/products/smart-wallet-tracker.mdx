## The Problem
I lose my wallet at least twice a week. Not stolen — just... misplaced. Between couch cushions, in yesterday's pants, on my desk under a pile of papers. The Find My integration is a genuine life upgrade.

## Design
The AirTag slot is the genius part. It's not a bulky attachment or a cheap sleeve — the wallet is designed around it. The AirTag sits flush in the middle, adding zero bulk. You barely notice it's there until you need it.

The genuine leather feels premium for $34.99. After a month of daily use, it's developing a nice patina. The minimalist design forces you to carry only what you need — 6 cards max, plus a few bills.

## The AirTag Experience
Precision finding on iPhone 14 Pro leads you directly to the wallet with an arrow and distance. It's saved me at least 3 "where's my wallet?" panic moments in the first month.

## The Downsides
AirTag is sold separately ($29), so the real cost is closer to $64. The leather needs conditioning every few months. And if you're someone who carries 12 cards and 50 receipts, this isn't for you.

## Bottom Line
For Apple users who misplace things (you know who you are), this wallet pays for itself in stress reduction. The design is thoughtful, the leather is quality, and the Find My integration is seamless. One of those "why didn't they make this sooner?" products.
